FAQ
Common questions
Is this therapy? ↓
No. Solace is a peer support platform, not a therapy or mental health service. Our listeners are trained student volunteers, not licensed therapists, counselors, or medical professionals. We do not diagnose, treat, or provide medical advice of any kind. If you're in crisis, please reach out to iCall (9152987821) or Vandrevala Foundation (1860-2662-345).
Who are the listeners? ↓
Listeners are university students who have completed our 20-hour training program covering active listening, emotional safety, boundaries, and when to refer users to professional support. All listeners are background-verified and approved by our admin team before going live.
Is my conversation private? ↓
Yes. Sessions are conducted on Google Meet and are never recorded by Solace. Your journal entries are encrypted. Listener session notes are anonymized. The only exception is if a listener reports immediate safety concerns — in which case we follow our safeguarding protocol.
Can I stay anonymous? ↓
You can use a nickname in your profile and in sessions. Your real name is only used for payment processing. Listeners will only know you by whatever name you choose to share.
What if I'm in crisis right now? ↓
Solace is not a crisis service. If you are in immediate danger, please call 112 or go to your nearest emergency room. For mental health crises, iCall (9152987821) and Vandrevala Foundation (1860-2662-345) provide 24/7 support in multiple Indian languages.
What does the AI reflection do? ↓
After a session, our AI (powered by Gemini) generates a private reflection based on themes you might explore in your journal. It identifies emotional patterns and suggests journaling prompts. The AI never diagnoses, never labels mental health conditions, and never provides medical or therapeutic advice.
Important: Solace is a peer support community, not a mental health service. Our listeners are trained student volunteers — not licensed therapists, psychologists, or medical professionals. We do not diagnose, treat, or offer clinical care. If you are experiencing a mental health emergency, please contact a qualified professional or emergency services.

Safety & Content Policy

Platform policy: Solace is not a mental health service. Any listener who provides diagnostic, clinical, or medical advice will be immediately suspended. All crisis situations must be referred to professional services.
🛡️

Safeguarding Protocol

Listeners report safety concerns → Admin reviews within 2 hours → Emergency services notified if required.

📋

AI Output Review

Gemini-generated reflections are filtered through a content moderation layer to prevent any diagnostic language.

🔒

Data Protection

All journals are client-side encrypted. Session metadata is anonymized after 90 days. DPDP Act compliant.

📞

Crisis Referral

Listeners are trained to identify crisis situations and immediately provide iCall and Vandrevala Foundation contacts.
